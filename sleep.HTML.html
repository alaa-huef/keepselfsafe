Importance of Sleep

Sleep plays a vital role in good health and well-being throughout your life. The way you feel while you're awake depends in part on what happens while you're sleeping.

Sleep helps support growth and development in children and teens. Lack of sleep can lead to long-term health problems, affecting how you think, react, work, and interact with others.

Healthy Sleep Habit:

Improving your sleep often starts with better daily habits, like building a consistent routine and paying attention to sleep hygiene throughout the day.

Recommended Sleep Hours by Age

Age Group Recommended Hours of Sleep
Newborn (0–3 months)14–17 hours
Infant (4–12 months)12–16 hours
Toddler (1–2 years)11–14 hours
Preschool (3–5 years)10–13 hours
School Age (6–12 years)9–12 hours
Teen (13–18 years)8–10 hours
Adult (18–60 years)7+ hours

Benefits of Sleep

Sleep supports brain function, physical health, and emotional well-being. It boosts memory, reduces stress, improves weight management, and strengthens the heart.

Ways to Improve Sleep

  • Maintain a consistent sleep schedule.
  • Avoid caffeine and heavy meals before bedtime.
  • Exercise regularly but not close to bedtime.
  • Make your bedroom comfortable and relaxing.
