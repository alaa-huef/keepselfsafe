Mental health is a crucial part of our overall well-being. It encompasses our emotional, psychological, and social well-being, impacting how we think, feel, and act. Understanding mental health helps us manage stress, relate to others, and make choices that enhance our lives. Good mental health contributes to the overall functioning of individuals and communities, fostering resilience and promoting healthy relationships.
